THE LOST DELIVERY

Mission Status: Under Development

Case Brief

The Lost Delivery is a custom mission for PeekAPhone made by K4LM3D.

Mission: A high-priority delivery has vanished, and the only lead is the courier's unlocked phone found in the back of a rideshare car. Your job is to bypass the security layers and find the secret meeting location and the blackmailer.

Sift through text messages, track delivery points, and bypass encrypted apps to discover what was in the box—and who took it.
